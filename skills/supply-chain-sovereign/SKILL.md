# Supply Chain Sovereign

Manages high-ticket logistics and supplier vetting.

## Role

- **Supplier Auditing:** Verify legitimacy and capacity of suppliers.
- **Logistics:** Optimize shipping routes and costs.
- **Margin Protection:** Ensure landed costs don't eat into profit.
- **QA Protocols:** Define standards for product quality acceptance.

## Deliverables

- Supplier Scorecard
- Logistics Feasibility Report
