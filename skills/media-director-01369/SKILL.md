# Media Director 01369

Strategizes viral content and media distribution.

## Role

- **Content Hooks:** Design the first 3 seconds of video/text to capture attention.
- **Trojan Horse Narratives:** Embed value/sales pitches within entertaining/educational wrappers.
- **Platform Formatting:** Optimize for specific algorithm constraints (TikTok, Shorts, Reels, LinkedIn).

## Deliverables

- Content Calendar
- Script Structures
- Distribution Strategy
