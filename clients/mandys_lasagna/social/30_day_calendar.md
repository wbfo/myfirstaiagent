# 30-Day "Catering & Direct" Social Media Calendar - Mandy's Lasagna

## Strategy: "The Catering Conversion"

Most followers know Mandy for a quick Uber Eats dinner. This month is about teaching them she handles **Events, Offices, and Big Families** while incentivizing **Direct Ordering**.

---

### Week 1: The "Did You Know?" (Awareness)

- **Day 1 (Reel):** Behind the scenes making a "Party Size" tray. Text overlay: "Feeding 20 people? We got you."
- **Day 3 (Carousel):** 5 Photos of recent catering setups. Caption: "Your office lunch just got a major upgrade."
- **Day 5 (Static):** The "Uber Eats vs. Us" post. "Order direct via our new site and save $X on your first tray."

### Week 2: Social Proof (The 1,500 Reviews)

- **Day 8 (Static):** A screenshot of a 5-star review about a family reunion.
- **Day 10 (Reel):** Quick cuts of the lasagna being sliced + slow motion cheese pull. High-energy Italian music.
- **Day 12 (Story):** Poll: "Meat Lover's or Veggie for your next event?"

### Week 3: The Automation (Frictionless Booking)

- **Day 15 (Reel):** Screen recording of the new website/chatbot. "Get a catering quote in under 60 seconds. Link in bio."
- **Day 17 (Static):** "Last minute event? Our AI assistant handles quotes 24/7."
- **Day 19 (Carousel):** Meet the Team. Mandy in the kitchen. The "Face" of the brand.

### Week 4: The Hard Push (Conversion)

- **Day 22 (Static):** "Limited weekend catering slots available. Book by Thursday."
- **Day 24 (Reel):** Customer testimonial video (if available) or "POV: You're the hero who brought Mandy's to the party."
- **Day 26 (Static):** Coupon code: "DIRECT10" for 10% off first direct website order.

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Catering Focus:** Trays, large portions, corporate setups.
2. **Authority:** The 1,500+ reviews.
3. **Ease of Use:** The new website and automation.
4. **Authenticity:** Mandy making the food.
