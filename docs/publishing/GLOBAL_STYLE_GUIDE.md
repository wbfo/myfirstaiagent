## 2. Visual Identity (Typography & Style)

- **Trim Size:** 6" x 9" (The Amazon Business Standard).
- **Body Text:** Serif font (e.g., Garamond) at 11pt or 12pt for readability and prestige.
- **Prompt Blocks:** Sans-serif font (e.g., Montserrat) in a gray-shaded box with a distinct border.
  - _Rule:_ All AI prompts must be clearly separated from the author's voice.
- **Headers:**
  - **H1 (Chapter):** Centered, Bold, Large.
  - **H2 (Section):** Left-aligned, Semi-bold.
  - **H3 (Sub-section):** Italicized or Small-caps.
